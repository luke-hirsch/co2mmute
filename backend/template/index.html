A mobility lab in three phases

Designed for workshops, classrooms, and urban labs that want to explore infrastructure trade-offs and emission impacts together.

1. Scenario Setup

Choose city maps, set the CO₂ budget, and invite participants. Agents receive their commute roles and initial resources.

  • + Configure agent counts, player slots, and optional map updates.
  • + Secure sessions with passwords or open them to the crowd.

2. Decision Rounds

Players pick transport modes for each agent and negotiate infrastructure upgrades under time pressure.

  • ◎ Share live previews of commute times and emissions before locking decisions.
  • ◎ Vote on map updates that change the playing field for future rounds.

3. Insights & Debrief

Evaluate the impact of every round: emissions, equity, and infrastructure resilience.

  • ★ Highlight success metrics and emerging bottlenecks.
  • ★ Export summaries to share lessons with stakeholders.

Built for collaborative planning

CO2MMUTE grew out of applied research at the intersection of urban design and cooperative games. It is optimised for educators, municipalities, and innovation teams that want an engaging way to test transport futures.

Live multi-player sessions

Host moderated workshops or let players explore asynchronously with automated round timers.

Transparent CO₂ accounting

All emission calculations and mode choices trace back to the underlying data and assumptions.

Flexible configuration

Adjust map layers, agent personas, and victory conditions without touching code.

Insight-rich summaries

Export concise round reports to PDF or CSV for quick follow-up and policymaker briefings.
